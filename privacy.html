Privacy Policy

Tend. — Last updated May 9, 2026

Tend. is built with privacy as a core principle. Your data belongs to you and stays on your device.

What we collect

Tend. stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Journal entries you write
  • Habits you create and your completion history
  • Mood logs
  • App settings and preferences
  • Step count data from Apple Health (if you choose to connect it)

Where your data lives

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework. Daren Irlen never has access to your iCloud data — it goes directly between your devices through Apple's infrastructure.

Apple Health & HealthKit

Tend. can read your step count from Apple Health if you grant permission. This data is used only to display your daily step count within the app. Tend. does not store HealthKit data beyond the current session, does not share HealthKit data with any third party, and does not use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect any data on our servers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not show ads
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone
  • We do not have access to your journal entries, habits, or mood logs

Deleting your data

You can delete all your data at any time from the Profile tab in the app. This will remove all local data from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, CloudKit data will also be deleted.

Children's privacy

Tend. is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
